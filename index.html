Imagine Dragons

Over the last half-decade, Imagine Dragons have emerged as one of the biggest and most compelling rock bands in the world.
Within a year of its June 2017 release, their third studio album EVOLVE went double-platinum,yielding three back-to-back
#1 singles: “Believer,” “Thunder,” and “Whatever It Takes.”In the midst of their Evolve World Tour—a 116 date trek that
sold over 1.7 million tickets worldwide—the multi-platinum,Grammy Award-winning band began working on their follow-up,
and ended up creating their most dynamic work to date.
